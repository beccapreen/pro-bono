🚨 Critical — Fix First

💳

No Online Donation Mechanism

The single biggest missed opportunity
The Pink Ladies have raised over $2M in 46 years — but there is currently no way to donate online anywhere on the site. Visitors who want to support the cause have no clear path to do so.

Wix natively supports PayPal, Stripe, and Wix Payments. A donate button on every page could be live within a few hours.
🔴 Critical ~2–3 hrs · Wix Payments / PayPal
📅

Stale Events Page

Last event listed: November 2025
The only event currently listed is the Christmas Bazaar from November 2025. It is now March 2026. Outdated event listings are one of the strongest signals of an unmaintained website — they actively undermine trust with new visitors.

Wix Events auto-archives past events. Upcoming 2026 events should be added immediately.
🔴 Critical ~1 hr · Wix Events App
📧

Gmail Address as Primary Contact

pinkladiescayman@gmail.com — undermines credibility for a 46-year-old registered NPO
For a registered NPO with a .ky domain, using a Gmail address as the primary contact creates an immediate trust gap — particularly for corporate sponsors, media, and grant bodies. A professional address such as info@pinkladies.ky or hello@pinkladies.ky is simple to set up via Google Workspace (~$6/month) or Wix's own email tools, and signals organisational maturity.
🔴 Critical ~30 mins · Google Workspace or Wix Email

🟠 High Priority

📊

No Visual Impact Statistics

$70,000 donated in 2026 — where is this on the homepage?
The $2M lifetime figure is buried in a paragraph. The February 2026 Cayman Compass article confirms $70,000 donated to 13 charities — this should be front and centre. Visual impact counters (animated numbers, icon grids) are proven to drive volunteer sign-ups and donations.
🟠 High ~2 hrs · Wix Animations
👩‍💼

No Team / Leadership Page

Who are the Pink Ladies? The website doesn't say.
There are no names, photos, or bios anywhere on the site. Research by Stanford Social Innovation Review identifies leadership transparency as a top trust signal for nonprofits. A simple Meet the Team page with photos and short bios would transform how the organisation feels to first-time visitors.
🟠 High ~2–3 hrs · Wix Team Page
📬

No Email Newsletter Sign-Up

The highest-ROI channel for nonprofits — currently absent
There is no mailing list, no "stay in touch" prompt, no email capture anywhere on the site. Research from Litmus (2023) shows email delivers $36 return per $1 spent for nonprofits. Wix Ascend includes built-in email marketing — a simple sign-up form could go live in under an hour.
🟠 High ~1 hr · Wix Ascend
📋

Membership Form is Off-Platform

Currently links to a Google Form — breaks the journey
The membership/volunteering sign-up redirects to an external Google Form. This breaks the user journey, looks unpolished, and means data is fragmented. Wix Forms can replicate this natively, keeping everything in one place and allowing automated confirmation emails.
🟠 High ~1–2 hrs · Wix Forms
📖

About Page Doesn't Tell the Story

46 years of history reduced to a list of charity names
The About page is currently a plain list of charitable causes — no founding story, no timeline, no human warmth. Yet the history is genuinely rich: founded in 1980 by Olive Miller MBE and Evelyn Andresen; a hospital coffee shop that ran for 33 years; a fashion show tradition since 1982; a Christmas Bazaar since 1983. This should be told as a story — with a timeline, archival photos, and founder acknowledgement. It would take content work but minimal technical effort on Wix.
🟠 High ~3 hrs · Content + Wix Timeline Widget

SEO Basics Missing

Invisible to local search
No meta descriptions, weak page titles, no Google Business Profile. A local Cayman charity should rank well for searches like "volunteer Cayman Islands" or "donate Cayman charity." Wix SEO Wiz guides this process step-by-step — no coding required.
🔵 Medium ~2 hrs · Wix SEO Wiz
📰

No Press / Media Section

Recent Cayman Compass coverage has nowhere to live
A February 2026 Cayman Compass article confirmed $70,000 in donations — but there's nowhere to feature this on the site. A press page with media mentions builds credibility and makes it easier for journalists to cover future work. Simple to build on Wix.
🔵 Medium ~1 hr · Wix Page
🖼️

No Photo Gallery / Archive

46 years of moments — none of them visible
Events like the Fashion Show, Christmas Bazaar, and community volunteering days generate great photos — but they live only on social media. A gallery section would bring the organisation to life visually and support volunteer recruitment. Wix Pro Gallery handles this beautifully.
🔵 Medium ~2 hrs · Wix Pro Gallery
✍️

Blog Needs a Consistent Cadence

3 posts in 2025 — either commit or convert to News
The blog has only 3 posts from 2025, the most recent from May. A rarely updated blog harms SEO and signals inactivity. Recommended: rename to "News & Updates," aim for one post per month tied to events and donations, and keep posts short with photos.
🔵 Medium Ongoing · Content strategy
